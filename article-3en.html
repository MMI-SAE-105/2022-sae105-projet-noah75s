Opening Death Note

Each episode of the Death Note series begins with an opening and ends with an ending.


The music I chose from the anime adaptation of the manga Death Note. This music is considered a classic of the manga universe. It fits perfectly with the universe of the manga "Death Note" thanks to its lyrics that perfectly represent the main character who has in his possession the Death Note.

This music was written and composed by the group NIGHTMARE which is a Japanese rock group and which is also one of the legends of this field. It consists of Sakito and Hitsugi on guitar, Yomi on vocals, Niya on bass, and Ruka on drums. The band also appeared in the film 20th Century Boys. The band name is given by Hitsugi, explaining that he wanted a scary band name in the visual styles.



The members were all influenced by Japanese artists of their time, and therefore started as a cover band, before composing their own songs. The concept of the band Nightmare is 'giantizm', and this word is used a lot in their songs. Giantizm' is derived from a character in the anime Doraemon, Gian (Giant, in French). Its slogan is what's yours is mine, what's mine is mine. Let's talk now about the music in its details, we can think that it was created on purpose for this anime so much the universe of the music and the manga are similar but it is not the case.
