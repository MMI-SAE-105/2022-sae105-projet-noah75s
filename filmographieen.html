Filmography

All Studio Ghibli films! Studio Ghibli Inc (株式会社スタジオジブリ, Kabushiki gaisha sutajio Jiburi) is a Japanese animation studio founded by Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata in 1985.


Castle in the sky


Sky pirates, 'Dora's gang', attack a flying fortress; they are looking for a 'flying stone' belonging to a young girl, Sheeta (or Shiita), who is held prisoner.


My neighbour Totoro


Tatsuo Kusakabe, a young anthropologist and non-tenured professor at a Tokyo university, arrives in the village where he will be moving with his two daughters, Satsuki, ten, and Mei, four. The girls happily discover their new home, which has not been lived in for some time, and the surrounding countryside where a gigantic camphor tree stands tall.


Kiki the little witch


Kiki is a young witch who has just turned thirteen. This is an important date in her family: traditionally, at this age, witches have to leave their parents and settle for a year in a new town to complete their training.


Princess Mononoké


The story takes place in medieval Japan (Muromachi era). Ashitaka, the prince of the Emishi tribe, a reclusive people in northern Honshū, is struck by a curse after killing Naggo, a boar god turned demon: his arm is animated by supernatural strength, but is eaten away by the demonic entity.


Chihiro's journey


LThe film tells the story of Chihiro, a ten-year-old girl who, on her way with her family to her new home, enters the spirit world. After her parents are turned into pigs by the witch Yubaba, Chihiro takes a job at the witch's bathhouse to find her parents and return to the human world.


Howl’s moving castle


An eighteen-year-old girl, Sophie, who works in her dead father's shop, meets a mysterious sorcerer named Hauru by chance during a chase. He takes a liking to her.
