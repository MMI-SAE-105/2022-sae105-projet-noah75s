Death Note

Death Note is a shōnen manga written by Tsugumi Ōba and drawn by Takeshi Obata.


Death Note is a manga written by Tsugumi Oba and drawn by Takeshi Obata. What is Death Note? It's the story of Light Yagami, a high school student like you at the moment, who is fed up with this world that he considers corrupt. But his life will change forever the day he meets the famous "Death Note". When he opens this notebook, he sees an instruction manual containing the sentence "the person whose name is written in this notebook dies". At first he is suspicious, as we all would be when faced with a book like this.


He decides to test the notebook and discovers that the powers of the notebook are real. This notebook was the property of a "God of Death" who dropped it on Earth for entertainment. Light Yagami then decides to use the Death Note daily under the name of "Kira" to exterminate the people he considers "evil" in order to build a perfect world.
